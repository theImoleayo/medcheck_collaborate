About Us

Who we are!

We believe safety is a right — not a luxury.

In a world flooded with counterfeit drugs, expired food products, and harmful cosmetics, we are building a powerful yet simple tool that puts the power of verification in every consumer's hands.

MedCheck is a platform that allows users to verify the authenticity of drugs, foods, drinks, and personal care items before purchase. Whether you're scanning a barcode, entering a NAFDAC number, or reporting a suspicious product, MedCheck helps you make safer choices in real-time.

We are proudly Nigerian, deeply connected to our local realities, and driven by a passion for public health, technology, and community awareness. MedCheck is more than an app — it's a movement to take back control from counterfeiters and put it where it belongs: in the hands of the people.

Mission Statement

To empower consumers with simple, reliable tools to verify the authenticity of drugs, foods, drinks, and cosmetics before purchase, helping prevent harm and saving lives through technology, transparency, and awareness.

Vision Statement

To become the leading digital platform in Africa for eliminating counterfeit products, fostering a world where every consumer can make safe, informed choices — confidently, instantly, and securely.

Core Value

We aim to build and maintain trust between consumers, producers, and regulators by ensuring transparent, accurate product verification.
